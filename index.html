What it does

A complete desktop experience.
Built from scratch.

One piece at a time — because that's what makes it fun. Here's what's in the ship already.

🎨

Four Themes

Galactic Byzantium · Quantum Verdance · Void Pearl · Rosefire Eclipse. Hand-crafted, not generated.

🚀

First-Boot Wizard

Pick your theme, packages, and setup right from the first screen.

🔄

One-Command Updates

xyvorra-update pulls everything. Rollbacks included.

⚙️

Custom Installer

Full UEFI and BIOS support. Old hardware deserves love too.

🔐

Custom Login Screens

Custom SDDM themes — because the first thing you see should look good.

🖱️

Multiple Desktops

KDE Plasma, Hyprland, Sway, Openbox — all on the roadmap.
