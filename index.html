Four Themes

Hand-crafted, not generated. Each one its own world.

Byzantium
Green Haze
Black Pearl
Rosewood
First-Boot Wizard

Themes, packages, and layout from the get-go. No config files — a clean GUI that handles it all.

Updates & Rollbacks

One command handles both. Push a bad update? No panic — just roll it back. xyvorra-update has you covered either way.

Custom Installer

Full UEFI and BIOS support. Gotta keep the old ships flying too.

Custom SDDM

Custom login themes because the first thing you see of the interior of your ship should look the part.

Hyprland & KDE

Support for both sides of the house — tiling and traditional, both welcome on this ship.
