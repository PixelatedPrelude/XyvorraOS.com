The story

So… why does this exist?

Honestly? I wanted to see if I could do it. I took a Linux class while working on my BA in Cloud Computing and Security — and it kind of snowballed from there. XyvorraOS is a one-person project: no team, no budget, just someone in school, working full-time in IT, somehow finding time to build a distro that looks like it belongs on a starship console.

If you're here you're either a tester, curious, or just stumbled in from the void. Either way — welcome aboard, crew member.

— Pixelated Prelude · Transmission end. ✦
