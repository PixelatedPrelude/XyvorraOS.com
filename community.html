The rules

Crew Code — the short version.

This is a chill project and the community around it should be too. There's only a handful of things that'll get you booted from the airlock.

🚫
No politics, religion, racism, or OS-bashing

Zero. Not the place, not the time, not ever.

🤝
Be decent to each other

Rude comments, personal attacks, being unnecessarily unpleasant — not here.

✨
Keep it clean

The vibe doesn't fit language or content that doesn't belong in a public space.

🌌
Don't spiral over internet stuff

Someone said something you disagree with? Scroll past it. Life's too short and the galaxy's too big.

The vibe is: good people, good conversations, good project. If something bothers you, move on. If something really bothers you, flag it quietly and let me know. That's it. Pretty simple crew code for a pretty simple goal — keep it fun.
