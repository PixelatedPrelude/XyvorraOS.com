Rules of the ship

Crew Regulations.

Short list. Easy to follow. Break them and you get booted from the airlock. It's not complicated.

XYVORRA-STATION · CREW-REGS-v1 · EFFECTIVE IMMEDIATELY
REG-01
No politics, religion, or OS-bashing
Zero tolerance. Not the place, not the time, not ever. This is a Linux distro, not a debate club.
REG-02
Treat crew members with basic decency
Personal attacks, being unnecessarily unpleasant, or making people feel unwelcome — none of that here. This is a chill ship.
REG-03
Keep it clean
Language and content that doesn't belong in a public space doesn't belong here either. Match the vibe of the place.
REG-04
Don't spiral over internet stuff
Someone said something you disagree with? Scroll past it. Flag it if it's actually a problem. Life's too short and the galaxy's too big.
REG-05
Use the right channels
Bug reports go in #bug-reports, not #mission-control. Feature ideas go in #feature-requests. Keep the station organized so everyone can find things.
The vibe is good people, good conversations, good project. This crew is building something real together — the OS, the community, the story around it. If something bothers you, move on or flag it quietly. If you're adding to the experience for everyone, you're exactly who this ship needs. Welcome aboard.
