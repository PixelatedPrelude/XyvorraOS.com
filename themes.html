// themes

Four themes.
Hand-crafted.

Not generated, not templated. Each color palette was designed to feel like it belongs in a different corner of the galaxy.

🪐
📂
⚙️
Galactic Byzantium

Deep purple-black space with Byzantium accents. Regal, dark, mysterious — like the far edge of a nebula.

🌿
📂
⚙️
Quantum Verdance

Bioluminescent green on deep black. Feels like a terminal that's actually alive — high energy, high contrast.

🌊
📂
⚙️
Void Pearl

The default. Deep ocean navy with cool blue-grey accents. Clean, calm, and built for long sessions staring at a screen.

🌋
📂
⚙️
Rosefire Eclipse

Blackout crimson — deep Rosewood on near-black. Intense, dramatic, and absolutely not subtle. For those who like danger.

🎨

Switch themes at any time

All four themes are included in every installation. Switch from the first-boot wizard or the Control Panel — no reinstall needed.
